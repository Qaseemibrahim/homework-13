wagyu ramen

Wagyu beef ramen isa luxurious twist on the classic Japanese noodle soup, featuring thinly sliced, high-quality Wagyu beef in place of the usual pork The rich, marbled Wagyu adds a deep, savory flavor to the broth, creating a truly indulgent experience. Often, the beef is cooked by the hot broth, resulting in a mix of rare and cooked bites as it simmers. The dish is known for its tender, buttery texture and umami-rich taste.
